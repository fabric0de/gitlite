# Security Policy

Please do not open public issues for sensitive security reports.

## Report Channel
- Email: `junghyeonkim.dev@gmail.com`
- Subject format: `[GitLite Security] <summary>`

## Include in your report
- Impact scope
- Reproduction steps
- Proof-of-concept (minimal, if available)
- Suggested mitigation (optional)

## Handling policy
- We will acknowledge and triage reports as quickly as possible.
- Valid vulnerabilities are handled privately until a fix is ready.
- A public advisory may be published after mitigation is released.
