# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful and inclusive collaboration environment.

## Our Standards
- Communicate with respect.
- Prefer evidence-based, constructive discussion.
- Welcome diverse backgrounds and perspectives.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Repeated bad-faith disruption
- Sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement
If you experience or observe violations, contact project maintainers.
Serious or repeated violations may result in warnings, access limits, or removal.
